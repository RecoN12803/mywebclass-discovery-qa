### Scenarios & Use Cases

**Scenario 1: Reviewing Submissions**
> Dr. Mitchell gets a Discord notification that 5 new design submissions have arrived. He approves 3 that show clear understanding and rejects 2 with notes.

**Scenario 2: Teaching with Examples**
> In his lecture on Brutalist web design, Dr. Mitchell pulls up MyWebClass on the projector. He shows the Brutalist gallery entry with its working demo and uses it to explain raw HTML aesthetics, intentional "ugly" design, and rejection of ornament. Students can see the principles in action.

**Scenario 3: Suggesting Resources**
> A student asks how to make their Swiss Design more authentic. Dr. Mitchell sends them to MyWebClass's Swiss Design page, which explains grid systems, Helvetica usage, and asymmetric layouts with a working example they can study.
